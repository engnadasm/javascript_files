Social Media Listening with Sentiment Analysis

Why it Matters?

Social listening is the process of monitoring social media channels for mentions of your brand, competitors, product, and more. Social listening gives brands an opportunity to track, analyze, and respond to conversations about them on social media. Itâ€™s a crucial component of audience research. Understanding how people feel about your brand helps you keep your marketing and product development efforts on track. It also allows you to respond right away to positive or negative posts.

Benefits of Social Media Listening with Sentiment Analysis

  • Engage with customers

    • Social listening gives you opportunities to engage with your customers about your brand. To find out their opinions about your product.

  • Track competitors

    • Social listening is more than understanding what people say about you. You also want to know what they say about your competitors. This gives you important insights into where you fit in the marketplace.

  • Discover new ideas

    • Social listening helps discover the problems that users face and how to solve these problems. Also, some users sometimes suggest a new ideas that may help you improve your product.
